# Product Guidelines: Financisto

## Communication Style
- **Professional yet Approachable:** User-facing text should be concise and professional to ensure quick information delivery, while maintaining a friendly and approachable tone to make financial management feel accessible.

## Brand Messaging
- **Financial Empowerment:** Every aspect of the app should reinforce the idea of helping users take control of their financial destiny through meticulous tracking and planning.

## Visual Identity
- **Modern and Familiar:** Adhere to Material Design principles to provide a consistent and intuitive experience for Android users.
- **Minimalist Data Focus:** Employ a clean and minimalist design language that reduces visual clutter, allowing the user's financial data to remain the primary focus.

## Error Handling and Notifications
- **Clarity and Action:** Error messages must be informative, clearly explaining what occurred and providing actionable steps for resolution.
- **Respectful and Unobtrusive:** Use unobtrusive notifications (like toasts or small banners) for non-critical updates to avoid interrupting the user's workflow.

## Internationalization (i18n)
- **Global Inclusivity:** Design with cultural neutrality in mind, using universally understood symbols and unambiguous terminology.
- **Robust Localization:** Provide comprehensive support for regional variations in currency, date, and time formatting.
- **Community-Driven Reach:** Leverage community-driven translations to ensure the application is accessible and accurately localized for users worldwide.
