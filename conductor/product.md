# Product Definition: Financisto

## Initial Concept
Financisto is an open-source personal finance tracker for the Android platform.

## Target Users
- **Individuals seeking detailed expense tracking:** Users who want to monitor their personal spending meticulously across multiple accounts.
- **Travelers:** Users who require robust multi-currency support to manage and convert different currencies while on the go.

## Goals
- **Total Visibility:** Provide users with a comprehensive view of their financial status across all accounts and categories.
- **Multi-currency Management:** Enable efficient tracking and conversion of expenses in various currencies.
- **Budgeting & Planning:** Facilitate the creation and maintenance of monthly budgets to encourage better financial habits.

## Key Features
- **Comprehensive Multi-currency Support:** Support for multiple accounts in different currencies with automated or manual home currency conversion.
- **Advanced Budgeting and Reporting:** Hierarchical categories and recurring budgets combined with powerful filtering and visual reporting tools.
- **Transaction Management:** Support for scheduled/recurring transactions, transfers, and split records to handle complex financial data.

## User Experience (UX)
- **Utility-Focused:** A functional and feature-rich interface that prioritizes financial utility and data density.
- **Reliable and Secure:** Ensuring data integrity through automatic daily backups and integration with secure cloud storage (Dropbox, Google Drive).
- **Mobile-First Efficiency:** Designed for quick data entry on mobile devices to capture transactions at the point of sale.

## Core Values
- **Open-Source Transparency:** Commitment to community collaboration and an open development process.
- **Performance and Efficiency:** Optimized for high performance and low resource consumption on Android devices.
